How to make a ham and cheese sandwich

  • Prepare the ingredients
    • Get two slices of bread.
    • Cut one slice of cheese.
    • Cut one slice of ham.
  • Assemble the sandwich
    • Place the slice of ham on top of one of the pieces of bread.
    • Place the slice of cheese on top of the slice of ham.
    • Place the second piece of bread on top of the cheese.
